Project Proposal

What is your website about?

A complete guide to Bangkok. Includes all the local eateries and unique places that foreign travel websites wouldn’t know about. Allowing the travelers to travel around Bangkok like a pro.

What is the objective of your website?

The objective of this website is to introduce Bangkok to travelers the way a local guide would. I want this website to accommodate those who are planning a trip to Bangkok, therefore, I want to include features like weather forecast and maps to allow the user to plan to get the most out of each location.

Who is your target audience?

My target audience are those who are visiting Bangkok and want a local glimpse of what Bangkok looks like and mean to us. In other words, those who want to see Thailand/BKK through our lens.

What content do you plan to include in each page of your website?

Home Page

For the home page we want to include a quick search function, which will minimize irrelevant browsing time. Below we want to include a carousel which offers monthly/weekly highlights. We also want to include comments and reviews from those who have used our platform for planning their trips, and maybe insert an official BKK video presentation.

1st Page: Restaurants

Recommendation for Restaurants. Including casual dining / Cafe / Tapas / Bakery / Homemade restaurants all over Bangkok. Each restaurant will have a tag which specify the location, therefore, the user could search based on its geographical location.

2nd Page: Shop

Places to shop around in Bangkok. Including the biggest / most interesting malls all the way to well known shopping streets or areas. Again, the user could specify the location and these activities would also come up.

3rd Page: Go

This page includes places to go and see. This will include seasonal events as well as places that you cannot miss when you are in Thailand. Places that are more traditional and mainstream but it’s a must.

4th Page: Night Life

This page will include recommendations for your night activities which may include drinking, karaoke or clubbing. Since this page is dedicated to travelers, I am planning to include bar with international music and places that welcome foreigners.

5th Page: Play/Do

This page will include all activities that are recommended for foreigners who are visiting. For example, the best places to get massages and for beauty / health purposes that may be expensive if you do it in other countries.
