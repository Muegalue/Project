Activities in Bangkok

There are many interesting activities you should do while you’re here. One of the things that you shouldn’t miss is getting a message. It could be anything from Thai Massage, aroma massage to massage candle. You can get 1 hour of very high quality massage for a more affordable price elsewhere. A traditional Thai massage is when the practitioner uses their hands, thumbs, elbows, forearms and sometimes feet to press and reduce tension in your muscle. It is an ancient practice that was used to help reduce body discomfort and also calm your anxieties and energize you. These are some places that we really like to go, although not all of them offer Thai massage.
